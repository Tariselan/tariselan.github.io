RAINBOW

How to go find a rainbow with Aaron.

What you'll need:

  • Visible Light
  • A Prism of some sort
  • An Open Space
  • A Blank Surface

Now, before you start making rainbows all willy nilly, please remember that rainbows are acutally optical illusions and you can find them just after it's rained.
Now that I've told you what you need to have and know, it's time that you find out how to make one.

  1. Find an area that has a constant stream of light and a blank surface
  2. Put you prism on the ground
  3. Line up your prism with the beam of light
  4. Put your prism in the beam of light and adjust it's rotation and position accordingly
  5. You should now have you're very own rainbow, and all to yourself
